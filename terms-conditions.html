1. Cancellation Policy

Cancellations by Parent:

  • Cancellations made more than 24 hours before the scheduled booking start time are free and incur no financial charge.
  • Cancellations made within 24 hours of the scheduled booking start time will result in a charge to the parent of 50% of the total booking cost. This charge is paid directly to the babysitter as compensation for the short notice.
  • Cancellations made less than 4 hours before the scheduled start time will result in a charge of 100% of the booking cost.
